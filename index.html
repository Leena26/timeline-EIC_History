Timeline

    British trading stations in India were all run by the East India Company.

    EIC [East India Company] set up trading posts in Surat.

  • 1639

    EIC set up trading posts in Madras.

    The battle of Plassay took place. The East India Compand vs Indian Army led by Prince Sirajud-Daulah.

    Indian soldiers working for the British in Meerut shot down many British soldiers.

  • 1858

    Peace was made and the EIC was taken away its liberty to run India. It was replaced with drect rule from British Government.
